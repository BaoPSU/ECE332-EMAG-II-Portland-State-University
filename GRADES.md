### Cutoff perspective

Standard letter grade cuts:
- A: 90+, B: 80–89.99, C: 70–79.99, D: 60–69.99

Even with a 0 on the final, the most-likely scenario (labs rolled into lecture course) puts the course grade at a **mid C (~73%)**. The "labs don't roll in" scenario drops it to a **low D (~67%)**.

In other words: you're already locked into at least a C in the lecture course no matter what happens on the final. To drop to a D, the lab grades would have to be ignored entirely.
